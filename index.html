THE WORKFLOW

Power where it matters.
Clarity everywhere else.

The solver stays technical under the hood. Cloth NeXt turns it into a workflow that makes sense from setup through playback.

01

Production Bake Companion

Follow real solver activity, system load and progress without losing control of Blender. Cancel safely when a scene exceeds its memory budget.

02

One shared simulation

Bake multiple deformables and colliders together, including Follow Animation pins.

03

Animated forces

Drive supported gravity, wind and air behavior from keyframe-ready Empty objects.

04

More than cloth

Experiment with Rod / Cable curves and volumetric Soft Bodies using the same focused interface.

05

Failures you can act on

Persistent logs and stable error codes turn a failed solve into a useful next step instead of a dead end.
