WHY CLOTH NEXT

Built for scenes where contact quality matters.

Layered garments, close-fitting cloth and animated characters expose the limits of simple simulation setups. Intersections, unstable attachment points and failed long bakes quickly turn a creative task into technical cleanup.

Cloth NeXt brings material setup, collision control, validation, solver execution and cache playback into one Blender-centered workflow.
