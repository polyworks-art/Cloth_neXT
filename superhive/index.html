DESIGNED AROUND THE ARTIST

Physics power without leaving Blender.

Set up materials, pins, collisions and quality from familiar Physics Properties. Cloth NeXt handles scene validation, solver communication, cache creation and playback behind one deliberate Bake workflow.

Multi-object Bakes let garments interact in the same solve. Follow Animation Pins keep waistbands, cuffs and attachment points driven by character motion while the free cloth remains under solver control.
