# Cloth NeXt Superhive FAQ

## Is the solver included?

No. Cloth NeXt installs or selects the supported official PPF release separately
and only after explicit confirmation.

## Why does the first setup require a download?

The Blender extension and external solver have separate ownership, licensing and
update lifecycles. Cloth NeXt never hides this boundary.

## Why did my Bake stop at 90% RAM?

The default RAM safety guard prevents an unresponsive workstation. Simplify the
scene or cautiously change the threshold in Cloth NeXt preferences.

## Why can an animated collider pass through the baked cloth?

Use Animated Collider mode and increase Motion Samples / Frame. Eight is the safe
baseline; fast or strongly curved paths often need 12–16.

## Is cable thickness taken from Curve bevel depth?

Not physically. Curve bevel is visual. Surface Offset provides one uniform
collision-radius approximation for the simulated centerline.

## What should I send to support?

Send the CNX error code and the report generated by **Export Privacy-Safe Report**.
Do not send a production `.blend` unless support explicitly asks for a reduced file.

## Can I remove failed Bake data?

Yes. Use **Scan Cache Directory**, then **Remove Invalid Caches**. Unverified legacy
PC2 files are intentionally not removed automatically.
